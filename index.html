    About Us

    We are young team with a strong passion to create revolution in Agriculture as it is today and build a better India for tomorrow. Our goal of this Document is to envision the future for the farm sector so that the farmer will have a respectable income and livelihood in the ensuring decade. Achieving equity across regions and farm groups along with growth is the Vision Statement of this Document. Therefore, an attempt towards bringing the neglected regions and crops into the mainstream. At the same time realizing the optimum potential of the promising high growth regions will remain a vital component of the delineated growth strategy.

    Marketing

    We are focus on Three marketing goals


    1. The farmersshould getthe prices clearly emerging out of market forces and not managed by cartels of traders.
    2. Market margins and price spread should be within the acceptable limits by the farmers.
    3. The entire process of marketing should be farmer oriented and provide the producer required marketing freedom.

    Market Intelligence Cell

    Market Intelligence Cell with databank of information on both domestic and global trends across product chains and sectors. The cell shall provide information on market arrivals, database of major traders, price behaviors, analysis of market trends, price forecasting, storage and marketing aspects, global benchmarking, brand development, promotion, contract farming, organized retailing, supply chain providers etc. The market intelligence cell can derive critical inputs and research support.

    Increased no. of Producers & Consumers

    Agricultural infrastructure enhances improvements in both producer as well as consumer surplus. Increase in the number of regulated market committees, increased availability of banking operations in rural areas, increased availability of transportation facilities prevent the middle-men and the money lenders from appropriating a substantial amount of producer and consumer surplus. It should be noted that the welfare of the producers and the consumers improves from the fact that increased infrastructural facility brings producers and consumers to one place where producer could get an higher price for his products and consumers could pay lower price for the same product.
